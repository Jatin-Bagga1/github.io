Privacy Policy

Welcome to Volunteer Connect. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of personal information that is received and collected by Volunteer Connect and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide a better experience for our users:

  • Personal Information: This includes your name, email address, and any other information you provide when signing up for events or volunteering opportunities.
  • Non-Personal Information: We may collect non-personally identifiable information such as browser type, IP address, and usage statistics.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide you with volunteering opportunities.
  • To communicate with you regarding upcoming events, opportunities, and other relevant activities.
  • To improve our website and user experience based on your feedback and usage data.

3. Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmitting data over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

4. Cookies

Volunteer Connect uses cookies to enhance the user experience on our website. Cookies help us analyze web traffic and customize content according to your preferences. You can choose to accept or refuse cookies by modifying your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to other websites. These third-party sites are not operated by us, and we are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any third-party site you visit.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date. Please review this page periodically for the latest information.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@volunteerconnect.com.
